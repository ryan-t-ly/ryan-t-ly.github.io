Glitch Art

I personally define the term glitch as something that is temporarily broken. All these 3 characters are heroes and are similar in a sense as they have had lost something which broke them, but they all came back stronger than before. I thought that it was suiting to make this into a series and I feel that you can interpret the series by how they all have a speed ability. These glitches were made using Audacity and Notepad++. editing photos using software not meant to edit photos.
